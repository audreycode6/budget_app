Welcome to BudgeTing!

A Python/Flask application backed by PostgreSQL that allows users to create and manage budgets across different time horizons (monthly or annually).

  • ✓ Create and edit budgets
  • ✓ Track gross income & deductions
  • ✓ Manage bills, savings, and debt
  • ✓ Compare gross income to net income

How It Works

1. Create a Budget

Set up your budget with a name, income amount, and choose between monthly or yearly tracking.

2. Add Budget Items

Categorize expenses like bills, groceries, savings, and debt. Track where your money goes.

3. Monitor & Adjust

See your remaining balance in real-time. Edit items as needed to stay on track.
